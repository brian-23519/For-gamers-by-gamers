Call of Duty is a fast-paced, first-person shooter game series that drops players into intense combat missions across different eras and battlefields. From gritty World War II campaigns to modern warfare and futuristic conflicts, each installment delivers cinematic action, immersive storytelling, and competitive multiplayer gameplay. Whether you're fighting alongside allies or going solo in epic missions, Call of Duty challenges your reflexes, strategy, and survival skills.

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game set in the Titanfall universe, where players form squads of three and compete to be the last team standing. Each character, known as a Legend, has unique abilities that add depth to gameplay and strategy. With its fast-paced action, dynamic movement mechanics, and emphasis on teamwork, Apex Legends offers an exhilarating experience for both casual and competitive players.
